# Arx Planora

**Planora** is a modern, open-source collaboration and project management platform built for clarity, speed, and flexibility.
<br>
It helps teams plan work, stay aligned, and integrate everything they use - without jumping between tools.

---

## What Makes Planora Different

Planora is designed to stay:
- Simple and fast
- Open and extensible
- Built for real workflows
- Friendly for developers and teams

No clutter. No lock-in. Just a clean workspace that grows with you.

---

## Project Status

Planora is in early access and **actively** evolving.
<br>
**Feedback** and **contributions** are always welcome.
